Spain

Palma, Mallorca, August 2010 - My first time going abroad was during August in 2010 when I travelled to the famous tourist destination, the Spanish island Mallorca, more specifically to the capital of the island Palma. There I've seen things like the local cathedral, the yachts harbour and jellyfish casually swimming right by the tourists. From the local beach you could also see the planes leaving the island which was definitely a view. To cool down, we also travelled to the local aquapark where I've seen lemon trees just casually growing right by it.
