Cambodia

Siem Reap, June 2019 - During June 2019 I flew to Siem Reap, one of the biggest city in Cambodia to see Angor Wat and surrounding temples. There I managed to ride the famous tuktuk to see the temples and after exhausting day at the temples, I walked over to the Pub Street - the biggest street in Siem Rea to try the local delicatessen like BBQ snake & a burger from a crocodile, which by the way, is definitely worth a try.
