Albania

Tirana, March 2020 - In March of 2020, I decided to go to the capital of Albania for couple of days where I had some traditional Albanian food (see below) at the top of the highest mountain in Tirana, Mount Dajti, where I took cable cart to. Later on I went to visit the central square of Tirana which is a home of national statues as well as National Museum of Albania. I was lucky enough to also try some local baked treats in the nearby bakery with addition of visiting the famous concrete pyramid.
