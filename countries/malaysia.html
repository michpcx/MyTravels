Malaysia

Penang, June 2019 - I travelled to Penang for over 17 hours but it was definitely worth it. The beaches there are taken straight from the wallpapers you would usually get on your phone. The local people provide boat rides to places like Turtle Beach or Monkey beach or you can decide to walk throught the jungle if you choose to. The world famous street art in Penang is truly astonishing and the food can't be described with words, thats for sure. There I also took some basic lessons of Malay language as well as met the locals for them to help me see as much of Malaysia as possible.

Kuala Lumpur, June 2019 - I was in the capital of Malaysia for few hours since I had a stopover there and from what I've seen, the Kuala Lumpur International Airport itself is basically a shopping mall. You can get there absolutely anything you can think of. Gym? Sure. Western food? Sure. A bath? Sure!
