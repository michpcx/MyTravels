France

Saint-Valery-sur-Somme, August 2014 - One of the first bigger road trips I took was to the sea front in France to small but popular town Saint-Valery-sur-Somme. Where there was a language barrier, we had to use good old hand sign language to make sure we orderder the right pizza. Turns out our hand signs were outdated as we didn't get the right pizza in the end. There's also some serious wine choice in pretty much any groceries shop.
